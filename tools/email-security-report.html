  • Review your Cloudways account for any unauthorized login attempts
Red Flags to Watch For:
  • Random strings in email addresses - Legitimate companies use consistent, clean email formats
  • Mismatched sender names and domains - "Defender" from a mortuary domain is suspicious
  • Unsolicited funeral/obituary notices - Common social engineering tactic
  • Urgency language - "Action required", "Secure link", "Password Changed"
  • Financial promises - "Bonus Disbursement" from unexpected sources
  • "Unverified" sender badges - Outlook flags these for a reason
Best Practices:
  • Never click links in suspicious emails - go directly to the website instead
  • Enable two-factor authentication on all important accounts
  • Regularly review your account security settings
  • Report phishing emails to your IT department or email provider
  • Unsubscribe from newsletters you don't read to reduce attack surface
