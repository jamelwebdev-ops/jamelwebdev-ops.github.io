Tip of the Month
Dev Tip
Speed Up Your Local WordPress Setup
Stop cloning full production databases for local dev. Export only the tables you need (posts, postmeta, options) and pair it with a sanitized users table. You'll cut setup time from 20 minutes to under 3.
